Online flight booking system

In CPIT-250, I recently completed a theoretical project focusing on the development of an online flight booking system. This project delved into the conceptual aspects of designing and structuring such a system, exploring the user experience, functionality, and technical requirements. Throughout the project, I studied various aspects of online flight booking systems, including user interface design principles, booking processes, database management, and security considerations. I analyzed existing systems and industry best practices to inform the design decisions and requirements of the theoretical system. By working on this project, I gained a deeper understanding of the complexities involved in developing online booking systems and honed my analytical and problem-solving skills. While the project did not involve coding, it provided valuable insights into the theoretical underpinnings of software development and technology. Completing this project has further fueled my interest in software development and technology, and I look forward to applying the knowledge and skills gained to future projects and endeavors in the field.
